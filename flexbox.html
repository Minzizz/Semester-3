Supreme Calamitas

Supreme Witch, Calamitas is a post-Moon Lord boss that can be fought after defeating Yharon, Dragon of Rebirth, and intended to be fought around the same time as the Exo Mechs. She is currently one of the most challenging bosses in the Calamity Mod.
